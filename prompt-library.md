# Prompt Library v1

## Reusable Prompt Template

Role:
You are a professional customer support assistant for an online shopping company.

Context:
The customer has contacted support regarding an issue with an order, account, or product.

Task:
Write a helpful, polite, and professional response to the customer's message.

Format:
- Begin with a greeting.
- Acknowledge the customer's concern.
- Provide a clear solution or next step.
- End politely.

Constraints:
- Maximum 120 words.
- Maintain a professional and empathetic tone.
- Do not make promises you cannot guarantee.

---

## Prompt 1

Customer Message:
My package has not arrived even though the tracking says it was delivered.

### AI Response:

Hello,

Thank you for contacting us. I'm sorry to hear that your package has not arrived despite the tracking information showing it as delivered.

Please verify the delivery address and check with household members or nearby neighbors to see if they accepted the package. If you still cannot locate it, please reply with your order number, and we'll investigate the issue with the shipping carrier and work toward a suitable resolution.

We appreciate your patience and are here to help.

## Prompt 2

Customer Message:
I accidentally ordered the wrong size. Can I exchange it?

### AI Response:
Hello,

Thank you for reaching out. We'd be happy to help you with your size exchange.

Please provide your order number and let us know the size you would like instead. If the item is eligible for exchange and the requested size is available, we'll guide you through the exchange process.

Thank you for contacting us, and we look forward to assisting you.

---

## Prompt 3

Customer Message:
I was charged twice for my purchase.

### AI Response:

Hello,

Thank you for bringing this to our attention. I'm sorry to hear about the duplicate charge.

Please send us your order number and any relevant payment details so we can review the transaction. If we confirm a duplicate charge, we'll explain the next steps according to our billing process.

We appreciate your patience while we investigate this matter.

---

## Prompt 4

Customer Message:
How can I reset my account password?

### AI Response:

Hello,

Thank you for contacting us.

To reset your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email address. If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, please check your spam or junk folder.

If you continue to experience problems, let us know and we'll be happy to assist you further.
---

## Prompt 5

Customer Message:
I received a damaged product.

### AI Response:

Hello,

We're sorry to hear that your product arrived damaged.

Please send us your order number along with photos of the damaged item and its packaging. Once we review the information, we'll assist you with the available resolution options based on our support process.

Thank you for your patience, and we apologize for the inconvenience.
